THE PROBLEM

Training fades. The job doesn't wait.

Research on the forgetting curve is blunt: most of what workers learn in the classroom or the headset evaporates within days — long before they perform the task for real.

What follows is predictable. Errors. Rework. Safety incidents. Slow ramp-up.

THE SHIFT

Smart glasses just became real.

Lightweight, all-day wearable displays with hands-free input have arrived. Meta Ray-Ban Display with Neural Band today. More platforms coming.

For the first time, guidance can live where work happens — in the worker's line of sight, not on a tablet in their pocket. Skill Reality is built for this inflection.

HOW IT WORKS

From expert knowledge to guided performance.

01 Capture

Expert procedures and existing training content are structured into guided, step-by-step workflows — once.

02 Guide

AI delivers visual and audio direction through the glasses — hands-free, in real time, in the worker's field of view.

03 Reinforce

Performance data flows back to close skill gaps, prove competency, and sharpen the next run.

USE CASES

Three ways guidance shows up on the job.

Post-Training Field Reinforcement

After VR or classroom training, workers get on-the-job prompts and step guidance that lock in what they learned — right when they first perform the task for real.

Remote Expert Assist

A senior expert sees what the worker sees and guides them through complex or rare procedures from anywhere — cutting travel and downtime.

Real-Time Hazard Identification

AI watches the environment through the glasses and flags risks — missing PPE, live equipment, unsafe positioning — before incidents happen.
