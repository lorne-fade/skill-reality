THE PROBLEM

Training fades. The job doesn't wait.

Research on the forgetting curve is blunt: most of what workers learn in the classroom or the headset evaporates within days — long before they perform the task for real.

What follows is predictable. Errors. Rework. Safety incidents. Slow ramp-up.

THE SHIFT

Smart glasses just became real.

Lightweight, all-day wearable displays with hands-free input have arrived. Meta Ray-Ban Display with Neural Band today. More platforms coming.

For the first time, guidance can live where work happens — in the worker's line of sight, not on a tablet in their pocket. Skill Reality is built for this inflection.
